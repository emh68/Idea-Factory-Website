Welcome to the Idea Factory!

At The Idea Factory, you can bring your ideas to life. Whether you're just starting or already have some skills, we offer two ways to help you succeed:

Classes

If you're new to something like 3D printing, programming or audio recording, you can sign up for our classes. These classes will teach you valuable skills and the things you need to know so you can start creating right away. Click the button below for more information about our current class offerings and upcoming courses!

REGISTER

Makerspace

Already have the skills? You can sign up for a membership or a day pass and have access to our makerspace which includes the use of our equipment & tools like:

  • 3D printers
  • Drones (racing & video)
  • Computers
  • Audio Recording Equipment
  • Video Equipment (editing & green screen)
  • Electronics stations
  • Hand Tools
  • more coming soon!

Our goal is to give you the tools and skills to create amazing things!
