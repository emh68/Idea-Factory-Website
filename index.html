Welcome to the Idea Factory!

At The Idea Factory, you can bring your ideas to life. Whether you're just starting or already have some skills, we offer two ways to help you succeed:

1. Learn New Skills
If you're new to something like 3D printing or audio recording, you can sign up for our classes. These classes will teach you everything you need to know so you can start creating right away.

2. Use Our Tools
Once you've learned the skills, you can become a member and use our equipment, like 3D printers, audio rooms, drones, and more, whenever you need them. Already have experience? You can take a quick skills check, and if you pass, you're ready to use the equipment!

Our goal is to give you the tools and skills to create amazing things!
