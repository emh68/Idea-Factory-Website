What is the Idea Factory?

The Idea Factory is a place where you can come and use our tools and equipment to build your ideas. Kind of like a library, but with tools instead of books and while you don't take the tools home with you, you do take your projects home. But what if you don't know how to use our equipment or you want to learn a new skill like programming? You can take classes at the Idea Factory to learn those skills.
